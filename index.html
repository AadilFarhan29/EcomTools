How the Advanced math works (explain it)

1) Fees (incl. VAT)
Referral = SP × referral% (+ VAT)
Pick & Pack = fixed (+ VAT)
Closing = fixed (+ VAT)

2) Amazon payout
Payout = SP − Total Amazon Fees

3) Profit
Profit (Wholesale) = Payout − Wholesale CP − PPC
Profit (Loaded) = Payout − Loaded CP − PPC

4) Percentages
Amazon take % = Total Fees ÷ SP
Margin % = Profit ÷ SP
ROI % = Profit ÷ Cost
